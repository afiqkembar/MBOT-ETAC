Accreditation Objectives

The objectives of ETAC are to ensure:

1. The graduates of the accredited engineering programs meet the minimum academic requirements to be registered as graduate engineer with BEM.

2. The Continual Quality Improvement (CQI) is being practiced by Insitutions of Higher Learning (IHLs). Accreditation may also serves as a toll to benchmark engineering programs offered by IHLS in Malaysia.
